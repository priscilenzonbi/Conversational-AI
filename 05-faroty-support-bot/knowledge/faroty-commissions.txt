Faroty commissions for crowdfunding vary by two criteria: the type of project being financed, and the geographical origin of the donors.
Entrepreneurial projects (startups, products): 10% on local donations in the CEMAC zone, 15% on international donations.
Solidarity and mutual aid (associations, social projects): 5% local, 10% international.
Local communities (community development): 5% local, 10% international.
Festive events (weddings, birthdays): 10% local, 15% international.
International fees include currency conversion costs and cross-border bank charges.
